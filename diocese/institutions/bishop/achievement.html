Achievement

Congratulations Dear Rev. Fr. Duggimpudi Vijaya Paul Reddy, the New Administrator of Warangal Diocese

This is to communicate to you that as our diocese of Warangal fell vacant due to the appointment and Installation of our Bishop Udumala Bala as the Archbishop of Visakhapatnam, the diocesan Consultors on the Direction of the Apostolic Nunciature held a meeting to elect an Administrator for the Diocese.

The Apostolic Nunciature in New Delhi confirmed the name of Rev. Fr. Duggimpudi Vijay Paul Reddy as the Administrator of the Diocese of Warangal, with effect from 5/04/2025. Let us wish Him our Congratulatory and Prayerful wishes and cooperate with him in his ministry!
